About me:

I am a typeface and lettering designer with a background in communication design. I specialise in creating typefaces, including variable fonts and extended character sets for Latin and Cyrillic scripts. I am interested in working with other writing systems. I created typeface based on historical sources with extended Cyrillic and Latin character sets. I worked on expanding other authors' type families to support Vietnamese and Cyrillic scripts. I collaborate as an independent external designer with type foundries.
